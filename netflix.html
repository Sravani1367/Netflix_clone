What is Netflix?

Netflix is a subscription-based streaming service that allows users to watch a wide variety of TV shows, movies, documentaries, and original content on-demand over the internet. It revolutionized entertainment by popularizing the concept of "binge-watching" and shifting media consumption away from traditional cable TV.
